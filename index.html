Hero's Diary

Keeping a diary is great for your mental health. Many people journal for various reasons: managing emotions, developing positive habits like gratitude and pride, reflecting on life events, preserving memories, tracking progress on projects and goals, or establishing a routine of self-accountability. Some even write to share their stories with others. Whatever your reason for journaling, we're here to help.

Hero's Diary is a multilingual smart diary service designed to assist you in keeping a journal. Using the latest technologies, this service offers features that a traditional paper journal can't, keeping you engaged and motivated. Here are just some of the tools available, which can be turned on or off according to your preferences:

  • Weekly and Monthly Reports: Get detailed reports of your activities and achievements, written in any style you choose, so you can focus on your thoughts without worrying about writing structure.
  • Voice Dictation: Dictate your journal entries and listen to the AI resonse for a hands-free experience.
  • Interactive Q&A: Ask questions to your own diary and receive insightful prompts.
  • Reflective and Guidance Prompts: Get prompts for various activities such as CBT exercises, and training to focus your mind on gratitude, pride, caring for others, and scientific thinking.
  • Social Sharing: Share selected entries with a close circle of friends to deepen relationships and maintain accountability for your goals.
  • Health Tracking: Monitor your physical and mental health.

Hero's Diary will never sell or disclose your diary to third parties and will only release general statistics.

You can try all features for free and decide later if you want to pay for the advanced features or continue using the diary with basic features for free.
